Full-Spectrum
Design &

Silhouette Architectural and Engineering Consultants was established in June 2025 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. Founded by Tarek Abousakher and Caspar Smeets — two professionals who met at KAFD in 2012 and bring decades of combined experience across the Kingdom's most iconic developments.

We offer the complete range of architectural and engineering services from inception to completion: from early concept design through design management, project management, and site supervision.

The Founders —
Expertise at the Core

A rare combination of Saudi local expertise and international architectural rigour, united by a shared vision for design excellence in the Kingdom.

🇸🇦

Tarek Abousakher

Architect · Partner · Business Development

With roots at Omrania & Associates on KAFD, Tarek transitioned to real estate and business development with the Raidah Investment Company (RIC), where he managed design, sales, and fit-out across residential, retail, and restaurant projects. In 2022 he expanded his remit into the private sector.

KAFD · Raidah Investment Company Medad Office Solutions Omrania & Associates
🇳🇱

Caspar Smeets

Architect · Partner · Chief Design Architect

Before arriving in the Kingdom in 2012, Caspar worked as architect and project manager for OMA, UNStudio, and EGM Architects in the Netherlands. He served as Chief Architect on KAFD, overseeing world-class practices including HLA, SOM, Foster+Partners, Zaha Hadid Architects, and RMJM. From 2019 through 2025, he led 'Special Projects' at Omrania as Chief Architect on King Salman Park.
